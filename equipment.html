  4. Cleats or turf shoes(depending on where you will play)

    5. You can play in sneakers but cleats or turf shoes ensure that you won't be as likely to slip and fall. Your shoes are important so choose wisely.

The most important thing for every single piece of equipment you aquire is your comfort.Do you feel you play well with the stick and are you able to speak with your mouthgaurd?
